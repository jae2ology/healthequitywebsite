Make Change By Emailing Your Representatives!

There are only a few ways to make change with structural racism in health. We could train doctors and healthcare professionals to be more empathetic towards ALL patients. We could create more informational websites to try and get more people to be aware of this problem.


However, the main way this problem will face any real change is by creating policies that force healthcare professionals to treat every patient with respect and trust. We can push representativies to make bills and be aware of this problem.


You can help by entering your name, county, and clicking the button to be given a prewritten email for your local representatives. All it takes is for you to click a button and send it out!
